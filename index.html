The bonobo is an endangered great ape that lives in the tropical forests in the Democratic Republic of Congo. It is — along with the common chimpanzee — the closest living relative to humans. It is known for being an extremely peaceful and social species, which often resolves conflicts with sex. Bonobos are vulnerable to habitat loss and hunting for bush meat.

Bonodopt was established to raise funding for those on the front line in the battle against the extinction of bonobos.

We're speaking of Claudine André and her inspiring team. They have been protecting the bonobos for more than 25 years and achieved many milestones along the way.

Our favourite: Claudine founded Lola ya Bonobo, which is the world’s only organization to provide a sanctuary for orphaned bonobos and successfully release bonobos back into the wild.
